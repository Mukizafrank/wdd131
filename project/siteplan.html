Rwandan Cultural Tourism

Site Planning Document

Site Name

Rwandan Cultural Tourism

This name highlights Rwanda’s rich cultural heritage and tourism attractions. It was chosen because the website will showcase traditions, art, music, cuisine, and cultural destinations in Rwanda.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide visitors with insights into Rwanda’s culture and tourism. The site will highlight traditional practices, cultural sites, festivals, food, and music, serving as both an educational resource and a guide for tourists interested in cultural experiences.

Scenarios

  • Which cultural festivals can I attend when visiting Rwanda?
  • Where can I find traditional Rwandan crafts, music, and art?
  • What are the must-see cultural heritage sites in Rwanda?
